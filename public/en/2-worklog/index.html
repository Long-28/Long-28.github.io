Internship Worklog

This is the 12-week record of my internship journey, AI Journey on the First Cloud Platform. Each week documents the AWS services I studied, the tasks I completed, the evidence I collected, and the lessons I learned.

How to read this log

The WK-01 to WK-12 milestones follow the order in which the work was completed. Open each week to see its objectives, activities, achievements, and lessons learned.

The journey starts with AWS accounts, IAM, and networking, then moves through databases, migration, operations, automation, security, containers, and application services. It ends with monitoring, backup, and reflection.

Each week is a small milestone; together, the 12 weeks form a map of professional growth in the Cloud.
