Events Participated

Community events became important parts of my learning journey. They connected workshop knowledge with real-world stories and conversations beyond my own project. In 2026, I attended two events in the First Cloud AI Journey series.

Events on the journey

  • AWS First Cloud AI Journey Community Day 2026 — 23 May 2026, Ho Chi Minh City. Topics included enterprise AI architecture, LLM uncertainty, CloudFront, Amazon Q, context engineering, and an AI hackathon.
  • Mini Meetup – First Cloud AI Journey — 6 June 2026. Topics included Docker, AWS WAF with machine learning, WebSocket multiplayer systems, teamwork, Neptune GraphRAG, and Cloud career development.

What I brought back

I collected ideas that could be applied to my project: design from the system need, treat security as a starting point, observe application behavior through data, and keep learning through community conversations. These events helped me see Cloud not as a list of services, but as a way for different roles to solve problems together.
